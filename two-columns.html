NOT JUST A MEMBERSHIP, A COMMUNITY

Dancing is not just a hobby; it is a lifestyle. It is a part of you, how you express yourself. It is a way of living.

But there are times when the unexpected happens and you are left with no other choice than to surrender and leave dancing for a long period of time, letting down not only yourself and your team, but also your plans and expectations for what could have been. What could have happened if I hadn’t injured myself. If I didn’t need so much time to recover. 

 What if we tell you that we can help you minimize the risk of injuries. We have been through this. We know what it feels like. We have been on that road, so you don’t have to. 

 We have created an online platform that aims to support and educate fellow hip- hop dancers. It includes a wide variety of online workshops with easy and natural stretching flows so you can incorporate into your dance routine and a forum where people with the same passion can share their stories and inspire each other. 

 We can help you learn more about your body and its needs. Learn to take care of it as a part of your dance routine without any external or time-consuming effort. 

 It is not only about preventing health risks but also about learning more about yourself and your most powerful weapon- your body. How to take care of it, when to give it the needed rest and when you can push your boundaries and evolve.

What we offer?

Free Ad-supported plan

With our free plan, you'll have access to 60% of our content. While you'll encounter advertisements during your browsing experience, you'll still be able to explore a wide range of resources. However, please note that your access to our online forum and community features will also be limited. 

Regular plan

With our regular plan you will enjoy the ultimate experience including unrestricted access to all our content, exclusive premium online workshops designed to enhance your learning journey. You gain full access to our online forum and community, where you can engage in open communication, share insights, and connect with like-minded individuals. This plan is ideal for those seeking a deeper level of engagement and support.

Student plan

This plan is specifically designed to support students in their academic pursuits and personal development. Our Student Plan offers the same extensive access as our Regular Plan but at a discounted rate tailored to students.
