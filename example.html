Who we are

GROOVE was founded by Breda University students Ralitsa Siderova, Maria Shtarbeva, Mihaela Rashkova and Preslav Yanev in 2024. As young entrepreneurs they realized how crucial, but underestimated injury prevention is among young dancers and went on a mission to create a community of ambitious hip-hoppers, to teach them more about their abilities and how to use this knowledge to move forward fearlessly.

Our company’s name is the slang word Groove, that conveys a sense of enjoying oneself, accompanied by a sense of rhythm or flow. Because we believe that everyone should be free to express themselves through dance, not confined by physical struggles.

Our vision

In a world where we take our bodies for granted, we believe in prioritizing our well-being and helping each other, only then can we move fearlessly.

Our core values:

Education

In order to feel better and be better, you need to learn to listen to your body. Be aware of what you are capable of and take advantage of it. We from Groove help our users understand the power of knowledge and teach them about physical health, nutrition and movement principles in practice at their own pace depending on their individuality and lifestyle. We prioritize non-formal education through multiple interactive workshops and open lessons in our monthly programs.

Health

Let’s address it. Health is the most important thing. And usually, it is what we as young people underestimate most. What we from Groove aim at, is to raise awareness about potential health problems like the risk of injuries or burnout at a young age. We know what it feels like when the unimaginable happens. When your goal suddenly appears out of reach. Let’s try to prevent it. There is a way out. By incorporating injury prevention techniques in your dancing routine, you become more resistant and confident in your abilities.

Support

We have all been in tough times in our lives. We need these hard moments to understand how capable we truly are. We go through them to become stronger. But in such moments what we need most is the support from people who understand, people who have been through this and know how hard it is. That is why we believe that through mutual support our community is stronger- mentally and physically.

Collaboration

Collaboration is the fundament of Groove. Groove is built solely to help passionate dancers enjoy their journey to the fullest while also connecting them with people on the same path. Our community of hip-hop enthusiasts all over the world has become bigger through mutual responsibility and collaboration. We communicate daily, share our personal stories, and support each other along the way. Become a member and experience it yourself.
