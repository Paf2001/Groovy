Who we are

GROOVE was founded by Breda University students Ralitsa Siderova, Maria Shtarbeva, Mihaela Rashkova and Preslav Yanev in 2024. As young entrepreneurs they realized how crucial, but underestimated injury prevention is among young dancers and went on a mission to create a community of ambitious hip-hoppers, to teach them more about their abilities and how to use this knowledge to move forward fearlessly.

Our company’s name is the slang word Groove, that conveys a sense of enjoying oneself, accompanied by a sense of rhythm or flow. Because we believe that everyone should be free to express themselves through dance, not confined by physical struggles.

Our vision

In a world where we take our bodies for granted, we believe in prioritizing our well-being and helping each other, only then can we move fearlessly.
